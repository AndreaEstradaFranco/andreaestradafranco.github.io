About

Andrea Estrada is a Chilean artist who graduated from Nossi College of Art (Goodlettsville, TN) in 2006. Being able to incorporate her illustrations into her designs has been one of her greatest satisfactions as an artist.

Her culture and working mainly for educational based projects have shaped her style into bright and colorful art, filled with messages of hope and positive content.
